Yossi Gandelsman

I am a PhD student at Berkeley AI Research, advised by Prof. Alexei Efros, working on computer vision and machine learning problems.

Previously, I was a member of the Perception Team at Google Research.

I completed my M.Sc. at the faculty of Mathematics and Computer Science of the Weizmann Institute of Science under the supervision of Prof. Michal Irani.
